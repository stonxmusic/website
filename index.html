About

It's been a long time coming but we're here. Two long term audiophiles and armchair producers Ollie Barron (Skor Unknown) and James Clarke (ASHEMA) have combined efforts to produce STONX and STONX MUSIC. STONX MUSIC is a content creation collaboration focussing on producing and teaching electronic music production and composition. We launched with a two track, hard Drum and Bass EP to get your ears drooling but there's much more to come in many, many ways.

We aim to create an interactive musical entity to aid and amaze listeners and artists alike, please lookout for more from us 2020 and beyond. This is a crazy big deal so please throw all the support you can behind this. ALL SUPPORT GREATLY APPRECIATED, WE LOVE YOU ALL LIKE PATRICK STEWART LOVES THAT LENG LENG. Stonx Music

STONX

BIO

Drum & Bass duo STONX (Ollie Barron & James Clarke) combined with the sole purpose of creating HARD hitting music. 2019 marked the release of their Debut mini EP "TACTICAL BASS RESPONSE UNIT" a Hard Hitting Drum & Bass EP full of thumping drums and wobbly basslines, aswell as their Original Christmas SONG " IT's CHRISTMAAAS" an onslaught of sleigh bells and BASS

ASHEMA

BIO

Slithering out of the pits of despair, thought dead by his own design crawls Ashema. Armed with only a Plague Doctor mask and gifted the fucking ability to chase the ghosts outta your blood.
It’s beats all round and this time it’s personal. As Ashema regularly confuses making music with the therapy he desperately needs it’s gonna be a downward spiral from Melancholic Moments of Majesty to Barely Channelled Turbo wreckage. A desperate last appeal to reason before the world Chernobs itself. Can be found melting down his AA chips into arrow heads for the coming societal collapse.

Skor Unknown

BIO

Hailing from the UK, Edm Producer and Multi Instrumentalist Skor Unknown, started his journey playing Bass and programming beats for E-Metal band SHELLSHOCK back in 2009. After Producing 2 Studio albums and extensive touring, SKOR UNKNOWN hunkered down in his studio to work on phat beats and wobbling rhythms, releasing 2 original EP's and various Remixes from early 2012.

Atlantis Dies

BIO

Atlantis Dies was born from years of education and experimentation in the sphere of electronic music. Under this current alias, the Sheffield based musician and record producer Carl Martin has taken the influences of the LA Beat Scene, Neurobass & Hip Hop . Adding to them a sprinkle of the cinematic and ambient. This sonic vision has manifested itself into his debut EP ‘Carris Seed Dreams’ — the first branch in the evolution of the Atlantis Dies’ sound.
